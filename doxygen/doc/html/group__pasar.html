Detailed Description

A module that gets the objects present in the OPC, and change their top down saliency according to their acceleration, appereance and disappereance.


The Bottom Up saliency is based on lumicroma and will integrate the visual component. Version:1.0

Description

A module that gets the objects present in the OPC, and change their top down saliency according to their acceleration, appereance and disappereance.
The Bottom Up saliency is based on lumicroma and will integrate the visual component.

Parameters

  • –name: name of the module
  • –parameterTopDownAppearanceBurst: score of saliency for an appereance
  • –parameterTopDownDisppearanceBurst: score of saliency for an diappereance
  • –parameterTopDownAccelerationCoef: score of saliency for an acceleration detected
  • –thresholdMovementAccel: threshold of an acceleration detection
  • –thresholdSaliency: under this threshlod, saliency is considered as 0
  • –pExponentialDecrease: Speed of the decrease of the saliency over the time (should be less than 1)

Input Ports

  • /pasar/rpc [Bottle] [default carrier:tcp]: Response port. Supported commands:
    • pointing on/off: launch or stop pointing detection
    • waving on/off: launch or stop waving detection
    • help
    • quit
